Welcome!


Welcome to our digital nature walk. Here you can explore surreal and fantastical
landscapes that exist only in the virtual realm.
Here the flora and fauna are not bound by the constraints of the physical world.
While this virtual world may not represent the real-world nature we know,
it offers a different kind of experience that is both visually captivating
and creatively inspiring. There are no objectives or goals in this game,
allowing you to simply wander and explore to your heart's content.

Use the arrow keys to move around.
